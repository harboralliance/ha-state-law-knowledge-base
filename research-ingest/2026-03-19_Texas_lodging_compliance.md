### Summary of Human Trafficking Training Resources for Commercial Lodging in Texas

#### **1. Legal Requirement:**
The Texas Legislature passed House Bill 390 (87th Regular Session) which mandates that the Office of the Attorney General (OAG) approve training for workers directly employed by commercial lodging establishments with 10 or more rooms. This requirement also encourages all lodging establishments in Texas to provide human trafficking training, regardless of size.

#### **2. Training Requirements:**
- **New Employees:** Training must be completed by new employees not later than the 90th day after hire.
- **Annual Training:** All employees must receive annual training.
- **Certificate of Completion:** The approved training provider must provide a certificate to each employee, which includes the date taken and course title. Operators must retain copies of these certificates.
- **Signage Requirement:** Commercial lodging establishments must display human trafficking signage.
