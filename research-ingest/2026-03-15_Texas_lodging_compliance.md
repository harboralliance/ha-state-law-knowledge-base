# Texas Commercial Lodging Human Trafficking Training Requirements and Resources

## Overview
The Texas Legislature passed House Bill 390, which requires the Office of the Attorney General (OAG) to approve training for workers directly employed by certain commercial lodging establishments. This annual training requirement applies only to commercial lodging establishments with 10 or more rooms but is encouraged for all lodging establishments in the State of Texas regardless of size.

## Training Requirements
1. **Approval**: All approved training must be completed annually.
2. **Completion**: New employees must complete the training within 90 days of hire and all employees must receive annual training.
3. **Certification**: Approved providers must provide a certificate of completion for each employee who completes the training.
4. **Documentation**: Operators must retain a copy of each annual certificate of completion for every employee.
